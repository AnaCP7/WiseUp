Where does WiseUp come from

In our project, we aim to create a learning platform where users can play and broaden their general knowledge, with a special focus on highlighting the often forgotten contributions of women throughout history. We noticed a significant lack of representation and awareness about women painters, artists, sculptors, and more during our own educational experiences. Therefore, we wanted to differentiate ourselves from other applications by providing a balanced and inclusive platform that pays special tribute to these remarkable women.

The idea for this project originated from our final internship in the southern region of Spain, specifically in Málaga. As two passionate individuals eager to learn about the achievements of women, we embarked on this journey. Our goal is to create an educational and enjoyable experience that celebrates women's accomplishments while fostering gender equality.

We believe that by shedding light on these often overlooked women, we can inspire and empower users of all genders to appreciate their contributions and understand the importance of diversity and inclusivity in history and art. Together, let's learn from the past and create a brighter future.
